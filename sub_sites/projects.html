1) Tick Tack Toe
A Classic game of Tick Tack Toe recreated by me, requires two players (it can be buggy).
2) Snek
I wanted to make a Snake, but by my own rules. Eat apples to fill your hunger, but beware, it will start to deeplet faster and faster. Remember to keep an eye out for a golden apples (to move, either use arrow elements on the board, or arrow keys on your keyboard).
3) Pistolero
Fun little game I made when being bored. To win just shoot the bad guys with your cursor for points and try not to shoot civilians or you will loose health, (use "R" to reload). All of the assets were made by me, (as you can see, I am not a graphic designer).
